Examples

E.G.1 Find a positive and a negative angle coterminal with a 55° angle. 55°−360°=−305° & 55°+360°=415° A −305° angle and a 415° angle are coterminal with a 55° angle.




E.G.2 Find a positive and a negative angle coterminal with a π/3 angle. π/3+2π=7π/3*π/3−2π=−5π/3 A 7π/3 angle and a −5π/3 angle are coterminal with a π/3 angle.
