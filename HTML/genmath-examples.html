2.)In 1992, there were 285 cell phone subscribers in the Philippines. The number of subscribers increased by 75% per year after 1992. How many cellphone subscribers were in the Philippines in 2003?

Given: Solution: Answer:

t=1992

A=285 -> 498.73

T=11 years

r=75%

3.)An adult takes 400 mg of ibuprofen. Each hour,the amount in the person's system decreases by 29%. How much ibuprofen is left after 6 hours?

Given: Solution: Answer:

A=400mg

r=29%

t=6 hours

Formula:P(t)=A(1-r)t

P(6)=400(1-0.29)6

=51.24

There are 51.24mg of ibuprofen left after 6 hours in a persons system.
